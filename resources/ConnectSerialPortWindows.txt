1. Open Putty
2. Connect USB Serial Port
3. Open "Device Manager"
4. Scroll Down to "PORTS (COM & LPT)
5. Take note of what COMX. Could be COM1, COM2, etc.

6. Go to Putty
7. Click "Serial" for "Connection type:"
8. Set the "Speed" to "115200"
9. Click "Open" and you will have a connection.
